FEATURE: Checkout Flow

REQUIREMENT:
A logged-in customer with items in their shopping cart should be able to complete a purchase.

DETAILED BEHAVIOR:
- Customer reviews cart items, quantities, and prices on the checkout page.
- Customer selects a shipping address (saved or new).
- Customer selects a shipping method (Standard, Express, Overnight).
- Customer enters or selects a payment method (Credit Card, Debit Card, PayPal, UPI).
- An order summary is shown before final confirmation.
- Customer clicks "Place Order" to confirm the purchase.
- On success, order confirmation page and email are sent.
- On payment failure, the user must be shown an appropriate error and allowed to retry.

VALIDATION RULES:
- Cart must not be empty to proceed to checkout.
- At least one shipping address is required.
- Payment details must be valid before order can be placed.
- CVV should not be stored after transaction.
- Order total must match cart total including shipping costs.

BUSINESS RULES:
- Promo codes can be applied at checkout for discounts.
- Out-of-stock items should be removed from cart before checkout.
- International shipping requires additional address verification.
- Minimum order value may be required depending on shipping method.
- Tax calculation depends on the shipping region.
