FEATURE: Appointment Booking

REQUIREMENT:
A registered patient should be able to book a medical appointment through the system.

DETAILED BEHAVIOR:
- Patient must be logged in to book an appointment.
- Patient selects: Doctor, Specialty, Clinic Location, Date, and Time Slot.
- Only available time slots should be shown for the selected date.
- Past dates must not be selectable in the date picker.
- Today's date should be selectable only if time slots are still available.
- If no slots are available for a selected date, an appropriate message must be displayed.
- Patient receives a confirmation notification (email/SMS) after successful booking.
- Patient can view, reschedule, or cancel their upcoming appointments.
- Cancellation is allowed up to 24 hours before the appointment time.

VALIDATION RULES:
- Doctor must be selected before picking date.
- Date must be today or a future date.
- Time slot must be available (not already booked by another patient).
- Insurance information (if applicable) must be provided.

BUSINESS RULES:
- Each patient can have a maximum of 3 active (upcoming) appointments at a time.
- Appointments cannot be booked on public holidays.
- Emergency appointments bypass the standard booking flow.
- Doctors can block specific time slots.
